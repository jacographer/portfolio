Foreclosure Sales in Louisville, KY (2016-2022)

Site Analysis of Preston Ave in Louisville, KY

Maps foreclosures, reduced lunch data, racial demographics, and rent gap data for an site along the Preston Avenue Corridor in Louisville, KY. Produced with QGIS in collaboration with Michael McCanless, Gregory Madden, and Connor Schatz.

Adapts a gentrification vulnerability risk assessment models from the city of Portland, OR and applies the method to the Preston Avenue Corridor in Louisville, KY. Produced with QGIS in collaboration with Michael McCanless, Gregory Madden, and Connor Schatz.
